Overview

The 18% additional allocation in the GRU Euro Triangulated deposits is broken down as follows:

  • 7% to build the 777 account.
  • 4% to pay on behalf of the bank due to default.
  • 4% for the bank to support community and small business.
  • 3% for the bank as profits.
